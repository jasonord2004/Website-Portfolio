About Me

Who am I . . .

RUTGERS'26, PROSPECTIVE SOFTWARE ENGINEER, AI ENTHUSIAST

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Jason Ordonez. I am a 3rd year student at Rutgers University-New Brunswick pursuing a double degree in Computer Science(B.S) and Cognitive Science and a double minor in Japanese and Philosophy.

My interests include software design and development, data analysis, data science, artificial intelligence, and machine learning.

A general career goal that I have is to dive into the intersection of technology and human cognition.

My Top Skills

Listed below are some of the skills I have and I am always eager to add more!

JAVA

I have over 4 years of software development experience with the Java programming language, creating a variety of different coding projects that include the backend of a data manipulation software to rewrite arrival and departure times of NJ Transit trains, various course guided projects utilizing essential data structures/algorithms, and many more.

PYTHON

C
