AUTOMATED STUDENT FRACTION STYLE CLASSIFICATION

For this project, my group received access to a 15,000+ dataset of images of student-drawn number lines. To achieve a model that best analyzes students' error types, our research goal was to compare two image processing AI tools, YOLOv8 and Detectron2, on this data. To set up the YOLOv8 model, we first manually annotated a subset of these images with their strategy choices and error types. We then annotated the images by drawing bounding boxes to prepare the images for our digit recognition model. Ultimately, the results show that there is no difference between the two models in terms of accuracy, but YOLOv8 had a significantly lower training time.
